Dawn Brownies

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 1/2 cups s.r. flour
  • 1/2 cup cocoa (use a little less)
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 1/2 sticks butter
  • 1/2 cup cocoa (use a little less)
  • 3-5 tbsp milk

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
  2. Melt 1 1/2 sticks butter in a 9x12 pan.
  3. Cool slightly.
  4. Beat 3 eggs.
  5. Mix together sugar, flour, and cocoa.
  6. Add butter and eggs to mixture.
  7. Pour into 9 x 12 pan.
  8. Bake for 25 minutes. (Check after 22)
  9. While warm ice with:
    • 2 cup 4 X sugar
    • 1/2 stick melted butter
    • 1/4 cup cocoa (use a little less)
    • 3-5 tbsp milk
  10. Cut with plastic knife.
