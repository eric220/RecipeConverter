Oven Jambalaya

Ingredients:

  • 2 Cups Uncle Ben's converted rice – do not substitute
  • 2 lbs raw shrimp
  • 1 lb smoked sausage, chopped
  • 1 can French onion soup
  • 1 can beef broth
  • 1 stick butter, sliced
  • 1 8 oz. Rotel tomatoes with green chilies
  • 1 8 oz. sliced mushrooms (optional)
  • ½ C chopped green onions
  • ½ C chopped parsley

Instructions:

  1. Put everything into large casserole. Cover tightly. Bake at 350 for about 1 hr. 15 minutes.
  2. Add water at about 30 minutes if too dry.
  3. Cover until the last 20-30 minutes.
  4. Can use ham or chicken. Amount of chicken/shrimp/sausage is flexible.
  5. I use a LOT more green onions and parsley. Usually add the ½ C of each to the original mixture, but I add another cup of each for about the last 20 minutes or so.
  6. I also use only half of the Rotel with ½ can regular tomato sauce because I don't like it spicy.
